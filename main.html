Admissions

Siksha 'O' Anusandhan University (SOA) offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines.1 The admission process is based on the students' performance in the Siksha 'O' Anusandhan Admission Test (SAAT) conducted by the university.124 The admission process starts on 07 May 2023, and the final deadline for submitting the SOA University Application Form 2023-24 is 05 June 2023 for the academic year 2023. The admissions are based on the entrance exam result and merit list.2 SOA also provides direct admission to certain programs based on merit and other factors.0 To apply for admission, students need to visit the official website of SOA University, register with basic details and generate login credentials, fill out the application form with all required information, and pay an adequate amount of the application fee


Admission Procedure

Welcome to SOA University's admission process. Here's how you can apply:

  1. Visit the official website and create an account.
  2. Complete the online application form with your personal and educational details.
  3. Upload the required documents, including transcripts and certificates.
  4. Pay the application fee online through the provided payment gateway.
  5. Submit your application and wait for a confirmation email.

If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact our admission office at +123-456-789 or admissions@example.com.

SOA University Clubs

CODE-X

The Codex Club at SOA University is a haven for programming enthusiasts. We explore various coding languages, frameworks, and software development methodologies. Join us for coding challenges, workshops, and collaborative projects.

DANZAA

The SOA University Dance Club is a vibrant community of dancers passionate about various dance forms including hip-hop, contemporary, and traditional styles. We hold regular workshops, performances, and competitions.

SOA FAB LAB

The Fab Lab SOA Club is a creative space where students can experiment with digital fabrication tools, 3D printing, laser cutting, electronics, and more. Join us to turn your innovative ideas into tangible prototypes.

PHOTO-SHOOT

The SOA Photoshoot Club welcomes photography enthusiasts of all skill levels. Join us to explore various photography techniques, composition, lighting, and post-processing. Capture moments and tell stories through your lens.

GDSC

The Google Developer Student Clubs at SOA University is a community of students interested in Google technologies. Join us to learn about app development, machine learning, cloud computing, and more through workshops and events.

CRICKET CLUB

The SOA Cricket Club is a gathering of cricket enthusiasts who share a passion for the sport. Join us to practice, play friendly matches, and engage in tournaments. Whether you're a seasoned player or a beginner, we welcome all.
