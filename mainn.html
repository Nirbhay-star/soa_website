Admissions

Siksha 'O' Anusandhan University (SOA) offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines.1 The admission process is based on the students' performance in the Siksha 'O' Anusandhan Admission Test (SAAT) conducted by the university.124 The admission process starts on 07 May 2023, and the final deadline for submitting the SOA University Application Form 2023-24 is 05 June 2023 for the academic year 2023. The admissions are based on the entrance exam result and merit list.2 SOA also provides direct admission to certain programs based on merit and other factors.0 To apply for admission, students need to visit the official website of SOA University, register with basic details and generate login credentials, fill out the application form with all required information, and pay an adequate amount of the application fee


Admission Procedure

Welcome to SOA University's admission process. Here's how you can apply:

  1. Visit the official website and create an account.
  2. Complete the online application form with your personal and educational details.
  3. Upload the required documents, including transcripts and certificates.
  4. Pay the application fee online through the provided payment gateway.
  5. Submit your application and wait for a confirmation email.
