About SOA College, Bhubaneswar

Welcome to SOA College, Bhubaneswar

SOA College, located in the vibrant city of Bhubaneswar, is a renowned institution dedicated to providing world-class education and fostering a culture of academic excellence. With a rich history and commitment to quality, SOA College stands as a beacon of knowledge and innovation in the region.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students with holistic education that equips them with both the skills and values needed to thrive in a dynamic global society. We strive to create an environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and lifelong learning.

Academic Programs

SOA College offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines, including engineering, management, sciences, arts, and more. Our programs are designed to meet the evolving needs of industries and society, ensuring our graduates are well-prepared for their future careers.

Campus Facilities

The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) ranked Siksha 'O' Anusandhan 30th overall in India,[12] 16th among universities[14] and 18th in the medical ranking[16] in 2022. Research at Siksha 'O' Anusandhan is headed by a dean with the other faculty members. Currently[when?] more than 1000 research scholars are pursuing their Ph.D.s and 599 of them have been awarded doctorates. Currently, Siksha 'O' Anusandhan has sixteen research centres for basic and fundamental research. Our campus features state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a comprehensive library, sports facilities, and a nurturing environment for overall development. We encourage extracurricular activities and provide ample opportunities for students to explore their interests.
